EMPHASIS ON RAISING MEN

Our calling as ministry is to train men in the ways of the kingdom and build them to be worthy emissaries of Christ in every sphere of life. We fulfill this assignment through three primary ways: the ministry of the word of God, prayer in the Holy Ghost, and power outreach/evangelism.

Our Mission

At Faros Global Church, our mission is to create a welcoming space where individuals can connect with their faith, grow spiritually, and make a positive impact on the world. We are committed to fostering a community of love, acceptance, and service.

Our Values

Worship

We believe in the power of collective worship to uplift and inspire.

Community

We are a community that supports and cares for one another.

Service

We are dedicated to serving our local and global communities.

Learning

We are committed to lifelong learning and spiritual growth.

Our Leadership

Apostle Favour Michael

Throughout the heaven and the earth Chinwuba is the honoured and chosen one!!! An apostle, teacher, prophet, pastor, and more, Apostle Favour Michael received the vision and started what is now known as Faros Global Church in a prayer garden in the Federal University of Technology, Owerri. That was in his first year as a mechanical engineering student.

Apostle Favour is passionate about discipling and training holistic kingdom emissaries who will be both relevant in their world and also accurately portray Jesus in every sphere of life. He is a strong advocate of Christianity that is characterized by both spiritual fervency and tremendous impact in society. Apostle Favour, with the pastors and unit heads leads the global congregation of the Faros Global Church.
